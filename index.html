Panacea

A Solution towards Engineering Problems

By

Mr. Girish Ayya

Avadhoot Automations

31st March 2023

10:30 am -5:00 pm

Coordinator Contact Info:
Amey Mohile +91 95125 99149
Ruthu Rajendra +91 70224 42783

Description: A workshop that develops BRULE (an acronym for Break the RULE) thinking and creative aptitude towards providing solutions for a plethora of industrial problems. The participants will develop skills for understanding problems and contextual learning. This workshop will enable the students to change their mindset. The sessions will take you through the following steps in arriving at distinct solutions
-> How to understand the problem?
-> Gather information
-> Analyze and Interpret
-> Form an explanatory hypothesis.
